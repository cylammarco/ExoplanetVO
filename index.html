Exoplanetary Virtual Observatory

Exoplanet Virtual Observatory (EVO) is an Android application that allows you to use your smart phone or tablet to simulate and then observe your own planetary system. Choose from different types of stars to be the 'Sun' of your system and then add planets on orbits around it. Then observe your system with two different techniques that astronomers use to detect real exoplanets: planetary transits and the radial velocity (or Doppler Wobble) of the system's star.
